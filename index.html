About the App

Built for Creators, by Creators.

Professional tools (Pencil, Liner, Brush) designed for creative flow and tactile precision. No lag, just pure creation.

Onion Skin

Reference previous and upcoming frames transparently to draw with perfect continuity and fluid motion.

Layer Panel

Keep backgrounds, characters, and annotations separate for non-destructive editing and absolute control.
