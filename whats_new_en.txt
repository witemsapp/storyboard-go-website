🎉 What's New in v1.0.1

Critical Fixes & Optimizations
• ISSUE-001 (SGO Import Sync): Resolved! New panels created or duplicated immediately after an .sgo import will now save correctly every time. The save system is now properly initialized from the moment you open your project.
• Landscape Mode: Optimized margins and layouts across all screens.
• Technical Limits: Introduced a 4096px scene size limit to ensure smooth performance on older devices without sacrificing your creative space.

UI/UX & Quality of Life
• Video Tutorials: New banners with direct links to YouTube tutorials to help you get started.
• Bug Reporting: Added a dedicated "Report a Bug" button directly in the Settings.
• Language Indicator: A new checkmark in the language selector shows your currently active language.

Core Features (v1.0.0)
• First public release of Storyboard GO!
• Full storyboard creation with layers and panels.
• Export to PDF, PNG, MP4, and SGO.
• Onion skin for animation previews.
• Import/export projects as .sgo files.
• Privacy and Terms update.
